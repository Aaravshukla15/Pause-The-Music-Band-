About The Band

"Pause" is a Gen-Z band formed in the year 2022 and known for there indie bollywood and multi genre performances. Pause is known for their insane set with different and improvised sounds making people groove and hoot for it. Pause also focus on performing originals collectively by Chinmay , Romil and Rachit . The young dynamic & promising musicians of this scene have received a lot appreciation for their originals as well as all covers that have used modern elements of music with a powerful fusion blends. PAUSE's rendition and has enchanted the entire crowd and engaged them to sing along with them. Pause has been delivering energetically smooth performances to PAUSE your chaos life and enjoy music.

Sound

Pause believes in experimenting and exploring new sounds and patches to compliment songs with there magical twists and diversified music sets . With there improvisation on covers and there originals adding punchy bass with electronic guitars , groovy and tight drums along with keys and wonderful extraordinary patches on it with beautiful flute as cherry on the top , they know how to attract and make people groove on it .
